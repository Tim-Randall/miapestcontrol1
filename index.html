Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a licensed termite inspector?

Yes, Scotty is a very experienced and fully licensed termite inspector, with the certificate III qualification. This is a separate, extra qualification beyond the basic pest controller qualification. Scotty has dealt with many different cases of termite infestation over the years, he can check your building thoroughly and deal with any termites if they are found. Don't miss a termite problem due to an inexperienced or under-qualified inspector.

Do you service businesses?

Yes, Scotty has served many businesses over the years and we are happy to help any business large or small.

Are your solutions permanent?

No pest control solution can be permanent without posing a serious health hazard for humans. Scotty uses the latest and highest standard chemical solutions suited for each pest and situation. But each of these chemicals has a degradation time period, and becomes ineffective after a couple of years depending on what it is.

Why "control" instead of "extermination"?

Termites, cockroaches and many other pests would out live humanity if their was a global disaster. They are very robust animals, this makes total elimination highly unlikely and unrealistic. We keep it real and achievable, and dramatically reduce pests to the point they are very hard to detect, but cannot guarantee 100% elimination.

Where do you service outside of Griffith?

We are based in Griffith, and we service 150km radius of Griffith.
